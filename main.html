Summary

Inception is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The movie follows Dom Cobb, a thief who possesses the power to enter people's dreams and steal their secrets from their subconscious. Cobb is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased as payment for the implantation of another person's idea into a target's subconscious. The film is renowned for its complex plot and stunning visual effects.
